<p><ul><li>Hypoid Gear</li><li>Worm Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skew gears market encompasses various types, primarily hypoid and worm gears. Hypoid gears allow for quiet operation and high torque transmission, making them suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Their unique tooth design facilitates smooth engagement. Worm gears, characterized by their screw-like structure, provide high reduction ratios and are ideal for applications requiring significant torque in a compact space. Both gear types are crucial in machinery, robotics, and automotive systems for efficient power transmission and motion control.</p></p>
