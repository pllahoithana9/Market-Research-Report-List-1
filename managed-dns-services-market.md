<p><p>Managed DNS services are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based DNS services offer scalable, flexible solutions hosted on remote servers, enabling users to access high availability and automatic updates without heavy infrastructure investments. In contrast, on-premises DNS involves managing DNS servers within an organization's infrastructure, providing control over configurations and data but requiring greater maintenance and upfront costs. Both options cater to varying business needs, with cloud solutions favored for ease of use, while on-premises appeals to those prioritizing security and control.</p></p>
