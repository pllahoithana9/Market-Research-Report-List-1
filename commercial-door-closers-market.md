<p><p>The commercial door closers market features various types, including surface applied door closers, concealed door closers, and floor springs. Surface applied door closers are mounted on the door surface for easy access and installation. Concealed door closers, hidden within the door or frame, offer a sleek appearance while maintaining functionality. Floor springs are embedded in the floor, providing a durable option for heavy doors, ensuring smooth closing and opening. Each type caters to different aesthetic and functional requirements in commercial settings.</p></p>
