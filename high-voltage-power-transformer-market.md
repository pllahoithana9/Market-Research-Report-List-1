<p><ul><li>Dry-Type Transformer</li><li>Oil-Immersed Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage power transformer market comprises two main types: dry-type transformers and oil-immersed transformers. Dry-type transformers, known for their safety and environmental benefits, use air for cooling and are typically used in industrial and commercial applications. Oil-immersed transformers, on the other hand, utilize insulating oil for cooling and are favored for their efficiency and reliability in heavy-duty applications, such as utility grids. Both types serve critical roles in voltage regulation and power distribution across various sectors.</p></p>
