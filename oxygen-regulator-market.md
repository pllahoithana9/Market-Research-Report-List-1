<p><ul><li>Piston Type</li><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Click Style</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oxygen regulator market is categorized into three main types: piston type, diaphragm type, and click style. Piston type regulators utilize a moving piston to control gas flow, offering precise pressure regulation. Diaphragm type regulators employ a flexible diaphragm for smooth pressure delivery, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Click style regulators feature adjustable settings with audible indicators, allowing users to easily select desired pressure levels. Each type caters to specific needs in medical, industrial, and recreational oxygen applications.</p></p>
