<p><p>The LED lamp beads market primarily includes two types: SMD (Surface Mounted Device) LED lamp beads and directly-inserted LED lamp beads. SMD LED lamp beads are compact, allowing for high-density circuit designs and are commonly used in modern lighting applications due to their efficiency and versatility. On the other hand, directly-inserted LED lamp beads are larger and are typically used in traditional light fixtures. Each type caters to different applications, influencing their adoption in various lighting technologies.</p></p>
