Mechanical weeding machines utilize physical methods, such as hoes or brushes, to remove unwanted plants without harming crops, promoting sustainable agriculture. In contrast, chemical weeding machines apply herbicides to control weed growth effectively, ensuring higher crop yields but raising concerns about environmental impact and food safety.
