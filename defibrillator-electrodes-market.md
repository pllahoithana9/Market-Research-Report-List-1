<p><strong>The Defibrillator Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implanted Defibrillator Electrodes</li><li>External Defibrillator Electrodes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The defibrillator electrodes market encompasses two primary types: implanted and external defibrillator electrodes. Implanted defibrillator electrodes are designed for placement inside the body, typically used with pacemakers or implantable cardioverter-defibrillators (ICDs) to monitor and correct abnormal heart rhythms. External defibrillator electrodes, on the other hand, are used in emergency situations and attach to the skin to deliver electric shocks through external devices, providing rapid intervention for victims of cardiac arrest. Both types play crucial roles in cardiac care.</p></p>
