<p><p>Straight and articulating boom lifts are heavy machinery used for elevating personnel and equipment to high areas efficiently and safely. Straight boom lifts feature a telescoping arm that extends straight out, providing direct access to vertical heights, making them ideal for straightforward lifting tasks. Conversely, articulating boom lifts have jointed arms that can reach around obstacles, offering greater flexibility and maneuverability in complex environments.</p><p>The Straight and Articulating Boom Lift Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.5% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand from construction, maintenance, and industrial sectors, where safety and efficiency in working at heights are paramount. The growing trend towards rental services for such equipment allows companies to minimize capital expenditure while accessing advanced lifting solutions. Additionally, the rise in urbanization and infrastructure development across emerging economies is further propelling market growth.
