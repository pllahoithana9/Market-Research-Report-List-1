<p><p>The ureteric stents market includes several types serving distinct medical needs. Double J tubes are common, designed for temporary urinary drainage. Silent stents feature a smooth surface for easier placement and reduced irritation. Opening stents, equipped with a mechanism to allow for controlled expansion, help maintain ureter patency. Polycyclic J tubes have a unique shape that offers flexibility and stability within the ureter, minimizing the risk of migration. Each type is tailored to enhance patient comfort and procedural efficacy in urology.</p></p>
