<p><p>The high voltage charge pump market is primarily segmented into dual supply/output and mono supply types. Dual supply charge pumps are designed to generate multiple high voltage outputs from separate input voltages, facilitating flexibility in applications like display drivers or laser diodes. In contrast, mono supply charge pumps operate with a single input voltage, simplifying design and reducing component count, making them suitable for compact devices. Both types cater to different requirements in various electronic applications, enhancing system performance and efficiency.</p></p>
