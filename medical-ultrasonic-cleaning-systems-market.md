<p><p>The medical ultrasonic cleaning systems market is primarily divided into two types: single tank type and multi-tank type. Single tank systems consist of one chamber where cleaning, rinsing, and drying can occur, making them suitable for smaller facilities or specific applications. In contrast, multi-tank systems feature multiple interconnected tanks, allowing for dedicated cleaning, rinsing, and drying processes. This versatility makes multi-tank systems ideal for larger medical facilities requiring higher throughput and efficiency in managing various instruments and materials simultaneously.</p></p>
