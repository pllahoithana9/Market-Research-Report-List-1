<p><p>The Automatic Batch Coding Machine market is categorized into two main types: contact and contactless. Contact machines utilize direct physical contact to print codes onto products, ensuring precise and high-quality marking. In contrast, contactless machines employ technologies like inkjet or laser to apply codes without touching the surface, enhancing speed and reducing wear on both the printer and the product. Both types cater to diverse industries, offering solutions based on specific coding requirements, production speeds, and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
