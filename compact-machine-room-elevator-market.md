<p><strong>The Compact Machine Room Elevator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Traction</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compact machine room elevator market primarily includes traction and hydraulic systems. Traction elevators use a system of ropes and pulleys, allowing for efficient vertical movement, ideal for mid to high-rise buildings. They typically offer faster travel speeds and greater energy efficiency. In contrast, hydraulic elevators utilize a piston driven by a hydraulic fluid, making them suitable for low-rise buildings. They require less space but usually have slower speeds and a more limited travel height compared to traction systems.</p></p>
