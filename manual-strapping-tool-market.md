<p><p>The manual strapping tool market encompasses various types designed for different applications. Mechanical strapping tools operate through a ratchet mechanism, providing a reliable and consistent seal without needing an external power source. Hand pneumatic strapping tools, on the other hand, utilize compressed air to enhance efficiency and reduce manual effort. Both types cater to industries requiring secure packaging, with mechanical tools ideal for lower-volume tasks and pneumatic tools suited for higher-speed operations, combining effectiveness with ease of use.</p></p>
