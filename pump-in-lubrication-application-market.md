<p><strong>The Pump in Lubrication Application Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Pump</li><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Rotary Vane Pump</li><li>Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the lubrication application market, various pump types cater to specific needs. Internal gear pumps utilize meshing gears to create voids, drawing in lubricant efficiently. External gear pumps feature two gears that mesh externally, providing a steady flow. Rotary vane pumps use sliding vanes within a rotor to generate suction and pressure, ideal for low to medium viscosity liquids. Screw pumps employ a pair of screws to move fluid, offering continuous flow and high efficiency, particularly for thicker lubricants.</p></p>
