<p><ul><li>Flip Chip</li><li>Embedded Die</li><li>Fan-in Wafer Level Packaging (Fi Wlp)</li><li>Fan-out Wafer Level Packaging</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor packaging market features several key types, each with unique characteristics. Flip chip technology involves mounting chips face down on substrates for improved performance. Embedded die packaging integrates chips within the substrate, enhancing miniaturization. Fan-in Wafer Level Packaging (Fi WLP) allows for a compact design with direct connections to the die. Fan-out Wafer Level Packaging expands the design space, supporting larger chips with better thermal performance. Other types include traditional approaches like ball grid array (BGA) and chip-on-board (COB), catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
