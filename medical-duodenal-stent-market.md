<p><p>The medical duodenal stent market is categorized into two primary types: polymer duodenal stents and metal duodenal stents. Polymer duodenal stents are flexible, lightweight, and often used for temporary applications due to their biodegradable properties. In contrast, metal duodenal stents, typically made from materials like stainless steel or nitinol, offer greater durability and are designed for longer-term use. Both types aim to alleviate obstructions in the duodenum, improving patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
