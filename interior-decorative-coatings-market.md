<p><strong>The Interior Decorative Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solvent-borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior decorative coatings market can be categorized into two main types: waterborne and solvent-borne coatings. Waterborne coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, resulting in lower volatile organic compound emissions, making them more environmentally friendly and easier to clean up. They also provide quick drying times and excellent color retention. In contrast, solvent-borne coatings rely on organic solvents, offering superior durability, resistance to staining, and adhesion, but may emit higher VOCs. Both types serve various aesthetic and protective purposes in interior design.</p></p>
