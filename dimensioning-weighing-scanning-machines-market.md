<p><p>Dimensioning, weighing, and scanning (DWS) machines are essential for accurately measuring packages in logistics. The static type operates when items are stationary, providing precise dimensions and weight measurements but requiring manual handling. In contrast, dynamic type systems measure moving packages on conveyors, offering increased efficiency for high-volume operations. Both types cater to different operational needs, with static systems ideal for lower throughput environments and dynamic systems suited for fast-paced, automated sorting facilities. This distinction impacts market dynamics and customer preferences.</p></p>
