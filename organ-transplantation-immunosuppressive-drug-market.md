<p><p>The organ transplantation immunosuppressive drug market is categorized into several types. Calcineurin inhibitors, like cyclosporine and tacrolimus, help prevent organ rejection by suppressing T-cell activation. Antiproliferative agents, such as mycophenolate mofetil, inhibit lymphocyte proliferation. mTOR inhibitors, like sirolimus, block a key regulatory pathway in cell growth. Antibodies, including monoclonal antibodies, target specific immune responses, while steroids reduce inflammation and immune activity. Each category plays a crucial role in enhancing transplant success and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
