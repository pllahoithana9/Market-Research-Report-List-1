<p><strong>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ceramic Electro Static Chuck market is primarily categorized into two types: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. The Coulomb Type utilizes electric fields to hold semiconductor wafers securely, providing high stability and precise positioning. In contrast, the JR Type employs a combination of electrostatic forces and dielectric materials, enabling enhanced wafer clamping without the risk of thermal distortion. Both types are crucial in semiconductor manufacturing, facilitating efficient processing and improving yield in chip production.</p></p>
