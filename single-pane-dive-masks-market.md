<p><p>Single-pane dive masks are categorized into two main types: full face and half face masks. Full face masks cover the entire face, providing a complete seal and allowing for easier communication and skin protection. They often include integrated communication systems. Half face masks, on the other hand, cover only the eyes and nose, leaving the mouth exposed. These masks are typically lighter and more compact, making them easier to carry, but may not offer the same level of comfort and protection as full face masks.</p></p>
