<p><p>The stabilizer for polymers market encompasses various types, each addressing specific degradation issues. Antioxidants prevent oxidative damage, extending polymer lifespan. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) combat photo-oxidation, maintaining durability against UV exposure. UV absorbers protect against harmful radiation, reducing yellowing and brittleness. Antiozonants guard against ozone-induced deterioration, crucial for outdoor applications. Organosulfur compounds offer protection from thermal degradation and environmental stressors, enhancing overall polymer resilience. Together, these stabilizers ensure polymers perform effectively across diverse applications.</p></p>
