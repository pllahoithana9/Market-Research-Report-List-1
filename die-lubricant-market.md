<p><strong>The Die Lubricant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lubricating Wax</li><li>Lubricating Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die lubricant market encompasses various types, including lubricating wax and lubricating oil. Lubricating wax, characterized by its solid form, is used to improve the surface finish and flow of molten materials in the die-casting process, reducing friction. On the other hand, lubricating oil is a liquid that provides enhanced lubrication, cooling, and protection against wear and corrosion during high-temperature operations. Both types play critical roles in enhancing production efficiency and extending the lifespan of die tooling.</p></p>
