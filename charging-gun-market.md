<p><p>The charging gun market is divided into two main types: DC charging guns and AC charging guns. DC charging guns deliver direct current, enabling faster charging by allowing higher power levels, making them ideal for quick charging stations commonly found in commercial settings. In contrast, AC charging guns use alternating current, typically providing slower charging speeds and are often used in residential installations. Each type caters to different user needs, ranging from rapid charging to more convenient, overnight solutions.</p></p>
