<p><p>VOC gas analyzers are essential tools for monitoring volatile organic compounds in various environments. The market primarily comprises two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID analyzers use ultraviolet light to ionize compounds, offering high sensitivity and real-time detection across a broad range of VOCs, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, MOS sensors rely on changes in electrical resistance to detect gas concentrations, providing cost-effective solutions for indoor air quality monitoring and general applications.</p></p>
