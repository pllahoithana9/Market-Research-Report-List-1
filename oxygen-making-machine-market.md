<p><p>The oxygen making machine market is primarily categorized into three types: molecular sieve, oxygen-rich membrane, and others. Molecular sieve technology utilizes porous materials to separate oxygen from air, providing high purity levels. Oxygen-rich membranes employ selective permeability to facilitate oxygen extraction, delivering concentrated output efficiently. Other methods may include cryogenic separation and pressure swing adsorption. Each type caters to various applications, including medical, industrial, and residential use, addressing specific demands for oxygen supply with unique operational advantages.</p></p>
