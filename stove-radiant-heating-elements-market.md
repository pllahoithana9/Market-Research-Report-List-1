<p><p>The stove radiant heating elements market comprises various types, notably ribbon and coil radiant heaters. Ribbon radiant heaters feature a flat, bent metal design that allows for even heat distribution and quick response times, making them efficient for cooking. In contrast, coil radiant heaters consist of coiled metal wires that heat up when electricity passes through, providing effective and direct heat. Both types are essential in electric stoves, offering varied performance and energy efficiency for culinary applications.</p></p>
