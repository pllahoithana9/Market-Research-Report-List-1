<p><ul><li>Mechanical Scalers</li><li>Power Driven (Oscillatory) Scalers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The periodontal scaler market comprises two primary types: mechanical scalers and power-driven (oscillatory) scalers. Mechanical scalers operate manually, utilizing hand instruments like curettes and sickles for effective plaque and tartar removal. In contrast, power-driven scalers employ ultrasonic or sonic technology, generating rapid vibrations to enhance cleaning efficiency and reduce operator fatigue. Both types are vital for periodontal therapy, aiding in the prevention and treatment of gum disease while ensuring patient comfort and optimizing dental health outcomes.</p></p>
