<p><p>The Absolute Pressure Transmitters market is segmented into two primary types: AP Type and GP Type. </p><p>AP Type transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, providing high accuracy for applications requiring precise pressure readings, such as aerospace and pharmaceuticals. </p><p>In contrast, GP Type transmitters measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, making them suitable for general industrial applications where variations in atmospheric pressure are accounted for. Both types are essential for process control and monitoring in various industries.</p></p>
