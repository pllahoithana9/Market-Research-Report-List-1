<p><p>The portal crane market consists of two primary types: gantry cranes and half-gantry cranes. Gantry cranes feature a full frame that supports the lifting mechanism, allowing them to move heavy loads across a wide area, commonly used in shipping yards and construction sites. Half-gantry cranes, on the other hand, have a support structure on one side, making them ideal for low-headroom environments and providing efficient space usage in warehouses and manufacturing facilities. Both serve critical roles in material handling and logistics.</p></p>
