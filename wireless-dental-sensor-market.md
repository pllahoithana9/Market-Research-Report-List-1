<p><p>The wireless dental sensor market comprises various types, including CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), PSP (Photostimulable Phosphor), and others. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption and faster image processing. CCD sensors offer high-quality images with excellent sensitivity, making them ideal for detailed diagnostics. PSP systems utilize phosphor plates to capture images, which are then scanned, providing a balance between image quality and convenience. Other emerging technologies and variations continue to evolve within this market segment.</p></p>
