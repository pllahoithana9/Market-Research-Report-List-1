<p><strong>The Stone Water Repellent Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stone water repellent chemicals market is primarily categorized into two types: water-based and solvent-based formulations. Water-based repellents utilize water as a solvent, making them environmentally friendly and low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs). They offer easy application and quick drying times. In contrast, solvent-based repellents contain organic solvents, providing deeper penetration and longer-lasting protection but may have higher VOC content. Both types are essential for enhancing the durability and aesthetics of stone surfaces while preventing water damage.</p></p>
