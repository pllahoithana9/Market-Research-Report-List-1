<p><p>The ultrafast laser precision processing equipment market is categorized into three main types based on pulse duration: short nanoseconds, picoseconds, and femtoseconds. Short nanosecond lasers are optimal for applications requiring moderate precision and speed, often used in cutting and drilling. Picosecond lasers offer improved precision and are suitable for photonic applications, while femtosecond lasers deliver extremely high precision and efficiency for advanced applications like micro-machining and material characterization, enabling intricate manipulation without significant thermal effects.</p></p>
