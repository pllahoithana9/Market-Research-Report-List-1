<p><p>The electronics design software market is categorized into three key types: Printed Circuit Boards (PCB), Integrated Circuits (IC), and Semiconductor Intellectual Property (SIP). PCB design software focuses on creating layouts for circuit boards that connect various electronic components. IC design software facilitates the development of semiconductor chips, enabling complex circuitry within compact forms. SIP involves pre-designed functional blocks that can be licensed and integrated into various designs, speeding up product development and enhancing innovation in semiconductor applications.</p></p>
