<p><p>Wall-mounted toilet paper dispensers are categorized into two main types: manual and automatic. Manual dispensers require users to pull the paper by hand, providing a simple design and cost-effectiveness. They are often refillable and can accommodate various paper types. In contrast, automatic dispensers offer a touchless experience, utilizing motion sensors to dispense the paper, promoting hygiene and reducing waste. These dispensers appeal to high-traffic areas where convenience and cleanliness are priorities, enhancing user experience while minimizing contact.</p></p>
