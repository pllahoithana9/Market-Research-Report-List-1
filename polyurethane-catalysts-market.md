<p><p>The polyurethane catalysts market is divided into two main types: amine catalysts and organic metal catalysts. Amine catalysts include aliphatic amine, alicyclic amine, alcohol compound, and aromatic amine catalysts, used to accelerate curing reactions in polyurethane production. Organic metal catalysts encompass carboxylic acid salts and metal alkyl compounds, facilitating the polymerization process through metal-based reactions. Both catalyst types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and efficiency of polyurethane foams, elastomers, and coatings.</p></p>
