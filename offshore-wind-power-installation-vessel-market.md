<p><p>The Offshore Wind Power Installation Vessel market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Self-Propelled Jack-Up Vessel**: These vessels can navigate independently to installation sites and deploy legs to lift themselves above water for stability during installation tasks. </p><p>2. **Normal Jack-Up Vessel**: These rely on tugboats for positioning and also use jacking systems to stabilize for operations but lack self-propulsion. </p><p>3. **Heavy Lift Vessel**: Designed for transporting and installing heavy turbine components, these vessels utilize specialized cranes to lift and place equipment accurately on offshore sites.</p></p>
