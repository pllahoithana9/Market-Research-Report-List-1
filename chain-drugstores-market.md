<p><p>Chain drugstores operate within three market types: Regular Chain, Franchise Chain, and Voluntary Chain. </p><p>Regular Chains are corporate-owned and operate under a centralized management system, ensuring uniformity in branding and product offerings. </p><p>Franchise Chains involve independent owners who operate under a brand's licensing agreement, allowing for local autonomy while adhering to brand standards. </p><p>Voluntary Chains are cooperative networks of independent drugstores that collaborate for purchasing and marketing, increasing competitiveness against larger chains while retaining individual ownership.</p></p>
