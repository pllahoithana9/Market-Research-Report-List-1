<p><ul><li>White Thermal Printing Film</li><li>Transparent Thermal Printing Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Direct Thermal Printing Film market primarily consists of two types: White Thermal Printing Film and Transparent Thermal Printing Film. White Thermal Printing Film is designed for applications requiring a clear, visible print against a light background, making it ideal for labels and packaging. In contrast, Transparent Thermal Printing Film allows for printing on a see-through substrate, which is suitable for applications needing aesthetic appeal or visibility of the underlying surface, like window stickers or product labels. Both types serve diverse market needs.</p></p>
