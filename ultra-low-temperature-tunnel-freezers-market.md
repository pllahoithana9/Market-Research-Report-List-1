The market is segmented into two main types: Single-Channel and Multi-Channel freezers. Single-Channel freezers offer a straightforward freezing process, suitable for smaller operations with lower volumes. In contrast, Multi-Channel freezers feature multiple freezing lanes that enable simultaneous processing of various products, enhancing efficiency and throughput in larger production environments. Each type caters to different operational needs and capacities.</p></p>
